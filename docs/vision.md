# CleanIndia Connect — Project Vision

## Project Name

CleanIndia Connect

## Tagline

Report. Raise. Resolve. Rebuild a cleaner India.

## Project Mission

CleanIndia Connect is a civic-tech sustainability platform that helps citizens report garbage and cleanliness-related issues in their local areas, share proof through images, generate formal complaint text, spread awareness, and build a community around the mission of making India cleaner and more sustainable.

## Problem Statement

Many public places in India face issues like garbage dumps, plastic waste, dirty streets, overflowing dustbins, open dumping, and poor waste management. People often notice these problems but do not know how to report them properly or how to encourage others to take action.

## Proposed Solution

CleanIndia Connect provides a simple platform where users can submit garbage-related reports, upload photos, describe the problem, generate a formal complaint message, view reports from others, share sustainability tips, and track cleanliness impact.

## Target Users

* Citizens who want to report garbage problems
* Students and volunteers working on cleanliness drives
* Local communities
* Sustainability-focused people
* NGOs and awareness groups
* College students building social impact projects

## Main Objective

To create a digital platform that connects people, problems, awareness, and action for a cleaner and more sustainable India.

## Why This Project Matters

This project is inspired by the belief that cleanliness is not only the government’s responsibility but also every citizen’s responsibility. Small actions like reporting garbage, using dustbins, spreading awareness, and motivating others can create a big social impact.

## Version 1 Focus

The first version will focus on reporting garbage issues, displaying reports, generating complaint text, sharing sustainability tips, and showing basic impact statistics.

## Future Scope

In future versions, the project can include map-based reporting, AI image detection, real government portal integration, volunteer clean-up drives, sustainable product listings, and advanced community features.
